Sacramental Enrolments for 2026 are now open

To enroll your child in out Reconciliation, First Eucharist or Confirmation program please complete the above enrolment form and return to Silvia Kinder (not to the parish office).

Our sacramental program involves a series of weekly preparation classes in the lead up to each sacrament. Children must be able to attend most of the preparation classes in order to be eligible to participate in the sacrament. If you have any concerns or queries please contact Silvia.
