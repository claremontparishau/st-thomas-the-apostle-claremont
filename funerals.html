After you have met with your Funeral Director, please contact the Prue Pupazzoni at our Parish Office here and you can make an appointment to see the Fr Wayne, to discuss your loved one’s funeral. Please also contact a funeral parlour to ensure their availability.

Funerals will be held from 10.00am – 2.00pm.

The Olive Grove

The Olive Grove was founded in 2009, this is a Memorial Garden for the parishioners of St Thomas the Apostle Church and their loved ones.

Ashes are buried, memorial plaques are blessed in our Olive Grove of Gethsemane with rosemary symbolising new life.

Memorial Plaques – $225

The burial of ashes is free for Parishioners and non-Parishioners.
